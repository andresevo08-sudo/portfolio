How this works.
  • Live prices come from Finnhub's free /quote endpoint. You provide an API key once (stored in your browser's localStorage, never sent anywhere except Finnhub).
  • Refreshes every 30 seconds during US market hours (9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET, Mon–Fri), every 5 minutes when closed. US holidays not detected — the page will keep refreshing on those days but prices won't change.
  • Cost basis (shares × trade price) is hardcoded from the PDF. If you add/remove positions or change cost basis, edit the POSITIONS array in this file's source.
  • Free tier is 60 calls/min. With 14 tickers refreshing every 30s, that's 28 calls/min — well within the limit.
  • This is not a brokerage account view. Live P/L assumes the trade-price column is your true cost basis. Doesn't account for dividends, fees, or splits.
  • Need a key? Sign up free at finnhub.io/register — takes 30 seconds, no credit card.
